# Salesforce Flow Automation

This document describes automation used in Salesforce for customer onboarding.

---

## Flow Process

Customer Signup from SaaS system
↓
Lead created in Salesforce
↓
Validation rules applied
↓
Sales notification triggered
↓
Customer Success task created
↓
Dashboard updated

---

## Purpose

Automation reduces manual work, ensures consistent onboarding, and improves speed of customer activation.
