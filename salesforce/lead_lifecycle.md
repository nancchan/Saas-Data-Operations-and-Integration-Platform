# Lead Lifecycle (Salesforce)

This document explains how customer data transitions through Salesforce after being created from the SaaS platform.

---

## Lifecycle Steps

1. Lead is created from API onboarding
2. Lead is validated by sales team
3. Lead is marked as Qualified
4. Opportunity is created for potential deal
5. Opportunity is converted to Closed Won
6. Account and Contact are created

---

## Integration Context

- API creates initial customer record
- CRM receives Lead object
- Sales team works Lead in Salesforce
- Converted customer becomes Account + Contact

---

## Purpose

This lifecycle ensures that SaaS onboarding data is aligned with Salesforce sales processes and revenue tracking.
